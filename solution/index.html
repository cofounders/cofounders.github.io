The Nitty Gritty

Step by step

  1. Team of techies builds software for startup

    • Techies: Work on interesting projects with the best people around
    • Entrepreneurs: Hire a full-stack team through the network, or just complement your existing team
    • Investors: Have confidence in a team with a proven track record

  2. Startup pays cash and equity to Cofounders

    • Techies: Access to equity in promising startups
    • Entrepreneur: Reduced cash burn-rate increases runway
    • Investor: Just a single entity joins the companyâ€™s cap table

  3. Cofounders passes cash on to team members

    Cofounders does not seek to make profit by taking commissions.
    Goal is to minimize overhead by sharing legal and admin expenses through Cofounders.
    10% fee is charged by Cofounders to cover expenses.
    Leftover profit is distributed back to the team members.

Equity Pool

Cofounders manages equity on behalf of techies
Techies: Cost and administration of holding equity is eliminated, everyone gets the same, fair terms
Entrepreneur: Easier to deal with a single, experienced entity as investor

Some of the equity goes into a pool with other startups

Techies earn from any profit the pool makes
